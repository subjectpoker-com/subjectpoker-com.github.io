Thomas Bakker

E-mail: thomas@subjectpoker.com
Skype: tm.bakker

Thomas will graduate from Leiden University in the Netherlands in 2011 with a BSc in Mathematics and a BSc in Computer Science. He wrote a book on poker strategy titled "Analytical No-Limit Hold 'em" that was published by Two Plus Two Publishing in December 2010.

Between August and December 2010, Thomas and Noah Stephens-Davidowitz worked with Cake Poker as an independent team to determine if anyone had exploited a security vulnerability in their poker software. They did this by developing, testing and applying statistical methods that analyzed the way players played to detect if anyone had more information than they were supposed to have.

Thomas co-founded Subject: Poker and was responsible for various things, including the technical side of running Subject: Poker. He has a blog at http://bakker.cc/blog/.
