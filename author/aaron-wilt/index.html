Aaron Wilt

E-mail: aaron@subjectpoker.com

Aaron Wilt, known as WiltOnTilt, is a high stakes no-limit hold’em poker professional who has been involved in the online poker community since 2004.

As a player, Aaron has earned over a million dollars from online high stakes no-limit hold’em cash games.

As a coach, Aaron has been an Executive Producer for DeucesCracked since its inception in 2008. He has created over 135 poker instructional videos and personally coached over 100 students. Aaron has also published strategy articles for Bluff Magazine and strategy segments for the 2+2 podcast.

As a blogger, Aaron took the lead in investigating the recent “Blue Monday” Maryland seizures. He was able to get answers for the community and keep people informed through his blog at www.PlusEVPoker.com and Twitter. More recently, he has launched a new division of his website, www.StolenBankrolls.com, where users can submit information related to their currently inaccessible funds for websites that have been unable to repay players.

Aaron looks forward to helping Subject: Poker provide quality news for the poker community.
